My dad introduced me to computers when I was in junior high when he brought home a Commodore 64 computer. As the years passed, even though I did not pursue a career in computers initially, I have kept up with certain aspects of them and how they are used in our world.

I love the creativity that coding is. I have always loved learning new things and expanding what I know and understand. In coding I get to continually learn and express myself in creative ways to build things. One of my hobbies is wood working. When I start to build a piece of furniture I design it, planning out the final product, then work backward to develop each step to bring the final product to fruition. In coding it is much the same. I design a final product, then work backward to develop the steps needed to create that product, and work at putting the pieces together to bring to fruition the original idea.
